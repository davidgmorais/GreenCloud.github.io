About us

Created in 2019 in a small town in Portugal, our platform's goal is to connect you to Nature through amazing and breath-taking experiences of your choice. We are an open window to the most inspiring views our mother Nature has to offer, all this without giving up on the comfort you love so much. You can now enjoy your trips without harming the ecosystem!

Widen your horizons

GreenCloud creates the possibility of booking, in a quick and simple way, a trip of your choice to places strongly connected to nature among the many options our platform has to offer.
We make nature accessible to you by creating unique natural immersions.
Among our options we provide you both free and paid experiences as well as an incredibly easy-to-use filter system, and also a rating system.

...and help us widen ours

In GreenCloud we value the opinion of our clients. With that in mind, if you have any knowledge of green spaces in your area that you would like to see in our web-site you can submit it to us. After a thorough analysis we add it to our data-base and it becomes automatically available.
